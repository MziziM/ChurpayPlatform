Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about Churpay's platform and services.

What documents do we need for church registration?

You'll need your church's CIPC registration certificate, banking details, and a bank confirmation letter. Our onboarding team will guide you through the entire process and help ensure all documentation is complete.

How long does the approval process take?

Most church applications are reviewed and approved within 2-3 business days. Complex cases or incomplete documentation may take longer, but our team will keep you informed throughout the process.

How do payout requests work?

Church administrators can request payouts through the dashboard. All requests go through our secure approval workflow, and funds are typically transferred to your verified bank account within 1-2 business days after approval.

What payment methods do you support?

We support all major credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), bank transfers, mobile payments, and recurring subscription donations. More payment methods are being added regularly.

Is our church's financial data secure?

Absolutely. We use bank-level security with 256-bit SSL encryption, PCI DSS Level 1 compliance, and regular security audits. Your data is protected by the same standards used by major financial institutions.

Can we customize the donation forms?

Yes! Professional and Enterprise plans include custom branding options, allowing you to match your church's colors and logo. You can also create custom fund categories and donation campaigns.
